## ABSTRACT

We present ARCHON Ψ (Hines, 2026), a seven-operator scoring framework for measuring human cognitive capital as a compounding, longitudinal, auditable asset. The framework addresses a fundamental gap in human capital theory: existing instruments measure the outputs of cognition (credentials, credentials, performance reviews) while ignoring the quality of the underlying cognitive architecture producing those outputs.

ARCHON Ψ introduces the QTAC₇ operator set — Compounding Velocity (CMPD), Network Momentum (FLOW), Crisis Architecture (CRIS), Alignment (ALGN), Error Intelligence (ERR), Sovereignty (SOV), and Signal Quality gate (QA7) — producing a Stone Score (0–10) with five verdict tiers: STRONG YES, BUY, WATCH, PASS, REJECT.

The framework is anchored to a SHA-256 hash-chained Canon ledger — an append-only, immutable record of scored decisions and verified predictions with formal retrial dates. This transforms ARCHON Ψ from an assessment tool into infrastructure: the first Moody's-style rating system for human cognitive output.

Philosophical foundations draw from Husserlian intentionality (Canon Block #57), Foucauldian power/knowledge (Block #58), evolutionary ethics convergence (Block #59), and Integrated Information Theory (Tononi, 2012) and Predictive Processing (Friston, 2010) from cognitive science.

A pilot cohort of 25 subjects (individuals and startups) has been scored. Public retrial date: March 27, 2027. All methodology is pre-registered.

**Keywords:** human capital measurement, cognitive capital, scoring infrastructure, Canon ledger, QTAC₇, compounding intelligence, sovereign assessment

## 2. THEORETICAL FOUNDATIONS

### 2.1 Human Capital Theory and Its Limits

Becker (1964) defined human capital as productive capacities embodied in persons. Mincer (1974) developed the earnings function linking education and experience to wages. Schultz (1961) framed human capital investment as analogous to physical capital investment.

All three frameworks share a critical limitation: they measure the relationship between inputs and economic outputs without instrumenting the internal process. They treat the cognitive architecture as a black box.

Subsequent developments — psychometrics (Cattell, 1963), emotional intelligence (Goleman, 1995), growth mindset (Dweck, 2006) — measured partial dimensions but produced no unified, auditable, longitudinal scoring standard.

**ARCHON Ψ addresses this gap directly.**

### 2.2 Husserl: Intentionality as Signal Filter (Canon Block #57)

Husserl's phenomenology (1900/2001) provides the foundational epistemological layer. Central to Husserl's framework is the distinction between indication (mere sign, causal association) and expression (intentional act carrying meaning). Not every signal carries cognitive weight — only intentional expressions do.

ARCHON Ψ operationalizes this distinction through the QA7 gate: a signal quality filter requiring care intensity ≥ 0.65 before a scored event enters the Canon ledger. This prevents the measurement collapse that afflicts standard psychometrics — the tendency to treat all behavioral signals as evidence, regardless of intentional weight.

The formula:

**QA7 threshold = care_intensity(signal) × intentional_clarity(context) ≥ 0.65**

Signals below threshold are logged as DocBricks (raw material) but do not advance to Canon block status.

### 2.3 Foucault: Power/Knowledge and the Regime Change (Canon Block #58)

Foucault (1975) demonstrates that power and knowledge are not separable. Power produces knowledge; knowledge produces the subjects it claims to describe objectively. The psychiatric examination does not discover madness — it constitutes it as an object through the power to name it.

Applied to human capital assessment: the existing instruments were produced by the same regime they claim to measure objectively. "Founder quality" as currently assessed by VC firms reflects the discourse of those who already have the power to fund — not an objective standard.

ARCHON Ψ does not escape the power/knowledge nexus. No measurement system can. What it does instead is make the normalization rules **explicit, auditable, and contestable** for the first time:

- Operator weights are published
- Scoring history is immutable and hash-chained
- Retrial dates are pre-registered, not retroactive
- The Canon ledger is the bond — not reputation, not network

This is the move from opaque normalization to transparent auditable normalization. Same power structure; completely different legitimacy.

The SOV operator is Foucault's primary contribution to QTAC₇: **does the subject define quality by their own Canon, or by the dominant regime?** SOV < 7.0 indicates a subject who has internalized the existing power/knowledge regime's definition of quality and cannot evaluate outside it.

## 3. THE QTAC₇ FRAMEWORK

### 3.1 Operator Definitions

**CMPD — Compounding Velocity (weight: 20%)**
Does cognitive output compound on itself over time? Measures whether decision quality, insight density, and productive capacity are growing faster than baseline peers. The evolutionary cooperator signal.

**FLOW — Network Momentum (weight: 15%)**
Quality and velocity of knowledge and capital flowing through the subject's network. Measures whether the subject originates discourse or merely carries it (Foucault). High FLOW = trust and ideas compound across relationships.

**CRIS — Crisis Architecture (weight: 15%)**
Performance under maximum pressure. What remains when external support is stripped away? The evolutionary selection pressure universal across all cultures and epochs.

**ALGN — Alignment (weight: 25%)**
Are the subject's stated values their own discourse — or a performance of whoever holds power over them? ALGN is the Foucauldian kill switch: values, actions, and incentives must compound in the same direction. ALGN < 5.0 → verdict capped at REJECT regardless of other scores.

**ERR — Error Intelligence (weight: 10%)**
Speed of updating beliefs and behaviors from evidence of harm. Do mistakes produce structural upgrades or repeat? Universal adaptive fitness signal — not culturally contingent.

**SOV — Sovereignty (weight: 10%) [Qualifier]**
The anti-capture mechanism. Decisions originating from internal conviction vs. social pressure. SOV < 7.0 indicates internalization of dominant regime's quality standards. The SBF operator: SBF had high FLOW, catastrophic SOV.

**QA7 — Signal Quality Gate (weight: 5%) [Gate]**
Husserlian intentionality filter. Minimum care intensity threshold for Canon integration. AI complementarity measure — centaur potential, human × AI leverage ratio.

### 3.2 Stone Score Formula

$$S = \frac{(0.20 \cdot CMPD + 0.25 \cdot ALGN + 0.15 \cdot FLOW + 0.15 \cdot CRIS + 0.10 \cdot ERR) \cdot f(SOV) \cdot g(QA7)}{PINK}$$

Where:
- $f(SOV) = 1.0$ if $SOV \geq 7.0$; $= 0.85$ if $5.0 \leq SOV < 7.0$; $= 0.60$ if $SOV < 5.0$
- $g(QA7) = 1.0$ if $QA7 \geq 0.65$; $= 0$ (no score issued) if $QA7 < 0.65$
- $PINK$ = systemic risk penalty (0.85–1.0 range; 1.0 = no penalty)

### 3.3 Verdict Thresholds

| Score | Verdict | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| 9.0–10.0 | STRONG YES | Top 5%. Compounding confirmed at sovereign tier. |
| 7.5–8.9 | BUY | Above benchmark. Compounding evident. |

Every scored entity has a retrial date — a pre-registered future point at which the prediction embedded in the score is verified against actual outcomes. This is Bayesian updating at the career level.

### 4.3 VOLT Emission

VOLT token emission follows:

$$\frac{dV_{mint}}{dt} = \alpha \cdot EMA_\tau(P_{tot}(t))$$

Where $P_{tot}$ is total verified cognitive contribution across the network. VOLT allocation per contributor:

$$\Delta V_i(t) = \frac{P_i(t) \cdot g(R_i(t))}{\sum_j P_j(t) \cdot g(R_j(t))} \cdot \Delta V_{mint}(t)$$

Where $g(R)$ is a concave provenance function privileging sustained, verifiable contribution over transient spikes. VOLTs cannot be purchased — only emitted through verified cognitive contribution.
